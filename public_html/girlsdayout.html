UCSD Women In Computing (WIC) is hosting its third annual outreach event, Girl's Day Out!

Are you interested in a career in Computer Science or Information Technology, but not exactly sure what it's all about? Get all your questions answered and more at Girl's Day Out 2015! You'll get to hear the experiences of industry, academic, and student speakers, see real-world applications of the field during lab tours, and have the chance to create a hands-on project, for FREE!

Make sure you mark your calendars, because this exciting event will be held at the UCSD Jacobs School of Engineering on Saturday, May 16th, 2015.

The event lasts from 10:00am - 4:00pm. Check-in begins at 9:30am.
